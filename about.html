Team Finished Final Album

Avicii’s untimely passing was a surprise for everyone. He was working on new music before his death, and afterward, his team announced that they put the finishing touches on the album for him, using everything that he had left behind.

The album, Tim, was released on June 6, 2019. The proceeds from the album were donated to the Tim Bergling Foundation, a non-profit, in Avicii’s honor, to bring awareness to mental illness.

Multiple Health Issues

Not many people know that Avicii suffered from a number of different health issues throughout his career. It was revealed in 2012 that he suffered from pancreatitis, from all of his partying and drinking. He also had a double surgery in 2014, to remove both his gallbladder and his burst appendix. All of these health issues really took a toll on him and his career.

Donated A Lot To Feeding America

Avicii had a big heart and recognized that he was a lot more fortunate than others. In addition to using his star power to raise awareness about important causes, and raise money for charity from touring, he also donated his own money. He donated $1 million dollars to Feeding America, a charity that fights hunger around the world.

Book Nerd

We only get a small glimpse into the lives of celebrities, and as a result, we only really get to see what they decide to show us or tell us. As for Avicii, he enjoyed reading and had even read all of the Game of Thrones books three times.

Retired In 2016

Having all of those health issues really impacted Avicii. He decided that he really needed to take a break, specifically from touring, and permanently stepped away from his hectic touring schedule in 2016. He announced that he would only be retiring from touring, but he would still continue to make music and better himself.
